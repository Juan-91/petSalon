Who Are We?

At Spaw Pet Salon we take grooming very seriously as it is a passion of ours to make every dog leaving the salon feeling a million bucks. We strive in having a relaxed, friendly and loving salon environment for every dog we groom. We pride ourselves in our attention to detail and care for your fur baby to make every visit to the salon enjoyable and relaxing.

We Specialize in Hand Scissoring, Breed Specific Grooming, Asian Fusion/Teddy Grooming, Hand Stripping

Ask our groomers about specific breed cuts. With attention to detail and an eye for flair, our groomers will have your pet looking like they're ready for a walk.
